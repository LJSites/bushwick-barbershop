The Shop

Clean, modern, and built on the cut.

Bushwick Barbershop sits on West 6th Street in the middle of Koreatown — a clean, modern room with a steady, lively energy and a bench of barbers who take the work seriously.

Owner Juan and the team deliver precise, real-quality fades day in and day out: skin fades, tapers, beard sculpting, and classic cuts. Regulars come back because the details are right and the time is respected. First-timers tend to become regulars.

Open eight to nine, seven days a week. Walk in, or call ahead and ask for your barber.
